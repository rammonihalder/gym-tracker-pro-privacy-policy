This Privacy Policy explains how Gym Tracker Pro handles your information. ### Information Collection Gym Tracker Pro does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information with the developer or any third party. The app does not require user registration or login. ### Local Data Storage All information entered into the app, including but not limited to: * Name * Age * Gender * Height * Weight * Body Measurements * BMI * Workout History * Diet Information * Fitness Progress is stored **only on your device**. The developer cannot access, view, or retrieve this information. ### Internet Connection Gym Tracker Pro does not require an internet connection for its core features. ### Offline Exercise Videos All exercise videos included in the app are stored locally and can be viewed without an internet connection. ### Advertising Gym Tracker Pro does not display advertisements. ### Analytics Gym Tracker Pro does not use analytics services. ### Third-Party Services This app does not use Firebase, Google Analytics, or any third-party service to collect user data. ### Children's Privacy Gym Tracker Pro does not knowingly collect personal information from children. ### Data Security Since all data remains on the user's device, users are responsible for protecting their device and any backups they create. ### Changes to This Privacy Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
