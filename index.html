Gym Tracker Pro

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 3, 2026

Thank you for using Gym Tracker Pro. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

Your Privacy Matters

Gym Tracker Pro does not collect, transmit, or share your personal information. All your fitness data remains stored only on your own device.

Information Collection

Gym Tracker Pro does not collect personal information from users. No registration or login is required.

Local Data Storage

The following information may be stored locally on your device:

  • Name
  • Age
  • Gender
  • Height
  • Weight
  • BMI
  • Body Measurements
  • Workout History
  • Diet Plans
  • Fitness Progress

This information never leaves your device unless you choose to back it up yourself. The developer cannot access your data.

Offline Functionality

Gym Tracker Pro is designed to work completely offline. Exercise videos included with the app are stored locally and do not require an internet connection.

Advertising

This application does not display advertisements.

Analytics

Gym Tracker Pro does not use analytics services.

Third-Party Services

This application does not use:

  • Firebase
  • Google Analytics
  • Cloud Storage
  • Advertising SDKs

Permissions

The app only requests permissions necessary for its features, such as notifications (if enabled) or access required for local functionality. No personal information is transmitted to the developer.

Children's Privacy

Gym Tracker Pro does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your own device, you are responsible for protecting your device and maintaining any backups.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated effective date.
